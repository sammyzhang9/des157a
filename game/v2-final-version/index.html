HOW LUCKY ARE YOU?

You will have to answer 4 questions correctly in a row in order to win, if you get one question wrong you will have to restart. The cards have numbers from 1-4 with the suits; diamonds, spades, hearts, and clubs.

Guess if the first card is red or black.

Guess if the next card is higher or lower than the previous card.

Guess if the next card is in-between the previous two cards or outside.

Guess the suit of the last card.

Game Over!
